\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A shape is used for collision detection. You can create a shape however you like. Shapes used for simulation in \doxylink{classb2_world}{b2\+World} are created automatically when a \doxylink{classb2_fixture}{b2\+Fixture} is created. Shapes may encapsulate a one or more child shapes.
