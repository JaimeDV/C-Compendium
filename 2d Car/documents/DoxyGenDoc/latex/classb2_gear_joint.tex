\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A gear joint is used to connect two joints together. Either joint can be a revolute or prismatic joint. You specify a gear ratio to bind the motions together\+: coordinate1 + ratio \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*} coordinate2 = constant The ratio can be negative or positive. If one joint is a revolute joint and the other joint is a prismatic joint, then the ratio will have units of length or units of 1/length. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
You have to manually destroy the gear joint if joint1 or joint2 is destroyed. 
\end{DoxyWarning}
