\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contact impulses for reporting. Impulses are used instead of forces because sub-\/step forces may approach infinity for rigid body collisions. These match up one-\/to-\/one with the contact points in \doxylink{structb2_manifold}{b2\+Manifold}.
